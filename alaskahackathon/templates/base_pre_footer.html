Hackawhat?

Mounds of data, amazing insight locked away in disparate data, an answer looking for its problem, a problem looking for a solution.


Non-profit companies in the Anchorage and Matanuska-Susitna area are looking for ways to utilize their data in a different way! Some may have a problem they are looking to solve, while others have large amounts of data, but want a way to utilize it.
